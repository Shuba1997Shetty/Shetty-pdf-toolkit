🔒 Privacy Policy

                
            

            

                
Last updated: May 2026

                
                
The Short Version

                

                    Shetty PDF Toolkit is a fully client-side application. 
                    All file processing happens entirely in your web browser. We do not collect, 
                    upload, store, or transmit your files or their contents to any server.
                


                
What We Don't Collect

                
    
                    
  • ❌ Your PDF files or their contents
    • 
                    
  • ❌ Bank statements, transactions, or financial data
    • 
                    
  • ❌ Account numbers, IFSC codes, or personal information
    • 
                    
  • ❌ Excel/CSV outputs you generate
    • 
                    
  • ❌ Your name, email, or contact info (unless you voluntarily contact us)
    • 
                


                
What May Be Collected

                

                    The site is hosted on GitHub Pages, which may log standard web access logs 
                    (IP address, browser type, page accessed). We do not have direct access to these.
                

                

                    If you contact us via email or WhatsApp, we will only have the information 
                    you choose to share with us.
                


                
Cookies

                

                    This site does not use cookies. Some browser-based settings (like your 
                    selected backup folder) are stored locally on your device only.
                


                
Third-Party Libraries

                

                    We use open-source JavaScript libraries (pdf-lib, pdf.js, SheetJS) loaded 
                    from public CDNs to perform PDF operations. These libraries run in your 
                    browser and do not send data anywhere.
                


                
Your Files

                

                    Your files stay on your device. When you "upload" a file, it's only loaded 
                    into your browser's memory for processing. Closing the browser tab clears it. 
                    No backup of your files exists on our side.
                


                
Children's Privacy

                
This service is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.


                
Changes to This Policy

                
We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here with a new "Last updated" date.


                
Contact

                
Questions about privacy? Use the "Report Issue" button at the top of this page.

👋 About

                
            

            

                
What is Shetty PDF Toolkit?

                

                    A free, browser-based suite of PDF tools — built especially 
                    for Indian users who deal with bank statements, GST documents, and everyday 
                    PDF work.
                


                
Features

                
    
                    
  • 🏦 Bank Statement to Excel — Supports SBI, HDFC, ICICI, Axis, Canara, Yes Bank, and 60+ other Indian banks
    • 
                    
  • 🔗 Merge PDF — Combine multiple PDFs into one
    • 
                    
  • ✂️ Split PDF — Extract specific pages
    • 
                    
  • 🗜️ Compress PDF — Reduce file size to specific target
    • 
                    
  • 🖼️ PDF ↔ JPG — Convert between formats
    • 
                    
  • 🔄 Rotate PDF — Visually rotate any pages
    • 
                    
  • 🔢 Page Numbers — Add numbers to pages
    • 
                    
  • 💧 Watermark — Add text/image watermarks
    • 
                


                
Why Free?

                

                    Indian users deserve good tools without paying foreign companies in dollars 
                    or worrying about uploading sensitive financial data to unknown servers. 
                    This was built as a free public tool to solve a real problem I faced myself.

Open Source-ish
